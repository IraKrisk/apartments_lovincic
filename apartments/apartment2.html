Reservation and Cancellation Policy

Deposit payments are refundable if the reservation is cancelled 30 days before original arrival date, minus administrative fee.


For cancellation less than 30 days before the original date of arrival:
- if we are unable to re-rent the apartment for the said dates, we reserve right to keep your deposit payment with the understanding that if we succeed in re-renting the apartment for the said days, we will refund accordingly.
- if we are able to re-rent the apartment for your original period of stay reserved, we agree to refund your deposit payment, minus administrative fee.


Contact us to make a reservation. We ask an advance payment of 20% of total amount to confirm your reservation.
