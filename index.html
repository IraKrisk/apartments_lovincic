Welcome!

Apartments Lovincic is a family run Holiday Home located in a quiet area of Baska, island Krk, only 200m away from the 2 km long Blue Flag beach, 800m from the town centre. We invite you to join us in one of the most beautiful resorts on the Adriatic.

We offer four apartments of different types to satisfy the needs of our guests.


Apartments type 4 + 2 are suitable for 4 - 6 people. You will have two double bedrooms, a living room with a kitchen, a bathroom, and a terrace. A double extra bed is available for larger groups.

Studio offers accomodation for two people. This small, cozy studio consists of a room with a kitchen, a bathroom and a terrace.

Each apartment has a parking space, air conditioning, Wi-Fi, a satelite TV and an outdoor grill included in the price.


We wish you an enjoyable stay in our apartments!
