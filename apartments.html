Apartments

Our apartments are suitable for 2 people or groups up to 6 people per apartment. Whether you enjoy your holidays with your special person, family, or friends, Apartments Lovincic offer you apartment to suit your needs.


All apartments are fully equipped, the price includes a parking space, air conditioning, WiFi, a satelite TV, a cooker, a fridge with a freezer, linen, towels, dishes, cutlery, everything you need to feel cozy.
