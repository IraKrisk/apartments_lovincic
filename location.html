Our Location

Baska is located on the south east coast of the Krk Island. Thanks to geographical position, it has excellent transport connections with most of European countries. There is a highway leading to the bridge of Krk which links island with mainland.


You can also use public transport. See the timetable of regular Arriva bus lines - Arriva


Airport Rijeka is situated 40 km away from Baska. It has very good connection with the rest of Europe and Croatian cities. Find your flight on Rijeka Airport


Ferry port Valbiska, situated 30km from Baska connects island Krk with the neighboring islands of Cres and Rab. Ferry timetable - Jadrolinija
